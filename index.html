From interest to reviewed plan.

The experience is designed to keep the medical gate clear: you submit information, a clinician reviews, and only appropriate patients move forward.

01

Private intake

Share goals, medical history, current medications, and location so the care team can route your request.

02

Eligibility review

A licensed clinician reviews fit, contraindications, and whether additional records or labs are needed.

03

Protocol decision

If appropriate, you receive a care plan with dosing guidance, expected follow-up, and pharmacy instructions.

04

Ongoing support

Check-ins help track tolerance, adherence, and whether the plan should be paused, adjusted, or continued.

Designed for responsible access.

Peptide care should feel accessible without skipping medical judgment. The first product is the review itself.

Good-fit signals

  • ✓You want a clinician to evaluate whether peptide care is appropriate.
  • ✓You can share accurate medical history and current medications.
  • ✓You are comfortable with follow-ups and stopping criteria.
  • ✓You understand that results vary and prescriptions are never guaranteed.

Safety filters

  • ✓Contraindications, allergies, pregnancy status, and relevant conditions are reviewed before a plan.
  • ✓Patients may be asked for recent labs, vitals, photos, or records depending on the request.
  • ✓Care is limited to states and jurisdictions where the reviewing clinicians and pharmacies can operate.
This site does not diagnose, treat, or prescribe by itself. Do not use peptides, GLP medications, or compounded products without guidance from a qualified healthcare professional.
