Patient flow

From referral to return care plan.

  1. Practice introduces the branded international access program to a qualified patient.
  2. Casanova conducts physician-reviewed intake and clinical appropriateness assessment.
  3. The patient receives a coordinated Southeast Asia program: institution, schedule, support, emergency protocol.
  4. Casanova manages in-country communication, logistics, documentation, and follow-up.
  5. Your practice receives a return care plan and de-identified aggregated intelligence over time.
Clinical posture

Access and navigation, not offshore prescribing.

Casanova informs, navigates, coordinates, and oversees access through licensed pharmacies, accredited hospitals, and physician-supervised programs. Your practice does not prescribe internationally. Casanova does not prescribe.

This page is partnership information only. Medical decisions remain with licensed clinicians and accredited institutions in the relevant jurisdiction.
